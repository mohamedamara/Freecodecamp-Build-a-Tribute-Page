Muhammad Ali

American professional boxer

Here's a time line of Muhammad Ali's life :

1942 - January 17, Muhammad Ali was born in Louisville, Kentucky. His name at birth was Cassius Clay.
1956 - He wins the novice Golden Gloves Championship as a light heavyweight.
1964 - February 25, he knocked out Sonny Liston. This defeat allowed him to become the heavyweight champion.
1971 - n March, in a famous match called "The Fight of the Century," Joe Frazier knocked Ali down, but Ali got up. However, after 15 brutal rounds, it was still ruled that Frazier won.
1976 - September 28, Ali beats Ken Norton in Yankee Stadium in New York.
2005 - He is awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom, the highest U.S. civilian honor.
2016 - Muhammad Ali died on June 3, 2016 as the result of septic shock due to unspecified natural causes. He was 74 years old.
